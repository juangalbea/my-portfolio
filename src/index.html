Who is Younger? Who is Older?

"Who is Younger? Who is Older?" is an app that lets users upload a photo containing multiple faces. The app detects the faces, calculates their ages, and determines who is the youngest and who is the oldest using a visually striking gradient from magenta to dark blue.

Easy Registration: Sign up to get started.

Face Detection: Upload a photo with multiple faces for automatic detection.

Age Comparison: The app identifies the youngest and oldest faces, highlighting them with a gradient scale.

Technologies Used: React, PostgreSQL, Node, Express, Clarifai API
